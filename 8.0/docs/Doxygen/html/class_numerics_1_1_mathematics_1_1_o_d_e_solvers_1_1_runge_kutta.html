Detailed Description

Runge-Kutta method for solving Ordinary Differential Equations (ODE).

Authors: Haden Smith, USACE Risk Management Center, cole..nosp@m.h.sm.nosp@m.ith@u.nosp@m.sace.nosp@m..army.nosp@m..mil

Description: In numerical analysis, the Runge–Kutta methods are a family of implicit and explicit iterative methods, which include the well-known routine called the Euler Method, used in temporal discretization for the approximate solutions of ordinary differential equations.
